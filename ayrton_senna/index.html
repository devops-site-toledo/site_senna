Resume

Ayrton Senna was born in March 21, 1960, was a Brazilian Formula One driver, and won three championships (1988, 1990, 1991). He's considered one of the best drivers off all time. He died in May 1, 1994 in a car accident during Imola Gran Prix, in Italy.
